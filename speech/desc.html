Speech Web Collaboration

This project was for the Web Development Project class. Conceptually it was the same as the TWIST project except the object here was to create a functioning website with a database for an actual client. Said client was the Communication Sciences and Disorders department of Kansas State University in Manhattan.

The client needed a site to aid in administering training to correct speech impediments, particularly those of the letter 'r', and to produce reports of patient progress. The project was to include a database with patients, clinicians, and flashcards used in the training sessions. The site would be made up of a login/logout system, a session and scoring system, the ability to add, edit and remove patients and clinicians from the database, and the ability to generate reports of patient progress.

Around halfway through this project, the COVID-19 pandemic caused the college campus to close and thus my group and I had to work from our homes. Thankfully, we made use of the remote meeting software Zoom to have regular meetings and work on the assignment together in real time.
