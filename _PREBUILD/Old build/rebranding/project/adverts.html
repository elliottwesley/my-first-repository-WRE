Satirical Advertisements and Web Banners

These are the advertisements I created to accompany my rebranding project: a billboard and three web banners. The theme I aimed to parody in these images is the belief/conspiracy theory that multi-billion dollar corporations are secretly attempting to control the general public via subliminal advertising (like other parodies of this kind, use of words like 'OBEY', 'SUBMIT', and in these images, 'CONSUME').


True, it's not likely that ads of this nature would be used by big-name franchises in the real world. But this idea was too darkly humorous to not consider!
