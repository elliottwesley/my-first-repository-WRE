Speech Web Collaboration

This project was for the Web Development Project class. Conceptually it was the same as the TWIST project except the object here was to create a functioning website with a database for an actual client. Said client was the CSD department of Kansas State University in Manhattan.

The client needed a site to aid in administering training to correct speech impediments, particularly those of the letter 'r', and to produce reports of patient progress. The project was to include a database with patients, clinicians, and flashcards used in the training sessions. The site would be made up of a login/logout system, a session and scoring system, the ability to add, edit and remove patients and clinicians from the database, and the ability to generate reports of patient progress.
