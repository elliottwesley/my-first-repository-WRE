Welcome to Web of Colors!

The purpose of this website is to explore a component of digital design that has fascinated me for several years: color, and more specifically the additive or RGB color model. This site comprises a brief explanation of what the additive color model is, followed by a study of what color gamuts (palettes) can be attained from parameters of ever-increasing size.

Use the links on the right, or above if you're on a mobile device or narrow browser window, to navigate the website.
