About Me

I've had an interest in computer applications since elementary school, and since then I've always wanted to turn that interest into a career. In particular, the programming aspect of computers has really caught my eye. I like to explore the possibilities and limitations of code, designing simple (and much less simple) applications and games.

As of May 2022, I am a graduate of Kansas State University Salina. Bachelor of Science in Engineering - Computer Systems.
